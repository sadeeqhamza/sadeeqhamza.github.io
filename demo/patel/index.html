Construction Management.

At J PATEL we offer extensive management services providing advice and project management within the construction industry. We can assist with the design, drawings, costing, programming and monitoring of a variety of construction projects on a short or long-term basis.The professional expertise we offer covers new constructions, redevelopments, relocations and contract administration. We are currently or have been involved in projects as diverse as town centre re-developments, residential schemes, and hospital projects. J PATEL helps her clients to meet the ever-increasing demands for better value and faster construction. .Our staff are highly experienced professionals and their varied construction backgrounds enables us to help with all manner of project situations even the unusual ones. . J PATEL can help you achieve your objectives of time, cost and performance.
