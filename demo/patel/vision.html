Our Vision

At J PATEL we expect to recreate standards in the Civil and building engineering sector. We proudly consider our quality control second to none and wish to maintain this by continuously improving on construction techniques and materials used to execute projects. It is our belief that this uncompromising quality of work will eventually maintain our name at the top of any list of construction firms. One of the main objectives at J PATEL is to make sure we live up to our social responsibilities by helping the educationally disadvantaged communities in which we operate, this is done by providing employment opportunities to the indigenes as well as social infrastructure to the host communities. It is our strong belief that by carrying out these objectives we will contribute positively to the growth of Nigeria in the 21st Century and in turn will lead J PATEL to greater heights in the construction industry.
